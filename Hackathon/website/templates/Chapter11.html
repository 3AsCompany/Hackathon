Comparing Quantities
Question 1

Area of a triangle with base (b) and height (h) is:

(a)5

(b)½ b.h

(c)20

(d)25

Answer: (b)

Question 2

Area of the square with side-length ‘a’ is:

(a)a2

(b)10

(c)15

(d)20

Answer: (a)

Question 3

The area of a parallelogram with length (l) and breadth (b) is:

(a) 1

(b) 2000

(c) 1b

(d) 4

Answer: (c)

Question 4

Area of a circle with radius ‘r’ is:

(a)5

(b)15

(c)πr²

(d)35

Answer: (c)

Question 5

1 cm is equal to how many millimeters?:

(a)10

(b)15

(c)20

(d)1

(b)5500

(b)6a²

(c)4

(d)6

Answer: (b)

(d)54

(a)60
