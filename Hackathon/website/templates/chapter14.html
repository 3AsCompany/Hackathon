Chapter 14 Factorisation

Question 1

The factors of 49p2 – 36 are:

(a) (7p+6)^2

(b) (7p-6)^2

(c) (7p – 6) (7p + 6)

(d) None of the above


Answer: (c)

Question 2

The factors of m2 – 256 are:

(a) (m + 4)^2

(b) (m - 4)^2

(c) (m – 4) (m+4)

(d) None of the above


Answer: (d)

Question 3

When we factorise x2+5x+6, then we get:

(a) (x +2) (x + 3)

(b) (x – 2) (x – 3)

(c) (x × 2) + (x × 3)

(d) (x × 2) – (x × 3)
