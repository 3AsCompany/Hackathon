Chapter 3 Understanding Quadilaterals

Question 1

Which of the following is not a regular polygon?

(a) Square

(b) Equilateral triangle

(c) Rectangle

(d) Regular hexagon


Answer: (c)

Question 2

If the two angles of a triangle are 80° and 50°, respectively. Find the measure of the third angle.

(a) 50°

(b) 60°

(c) 70°

(d) 80°


Answer: (a)

Question 3

In a parallelogram ABCD, angle A and angle B are in the ratio 1:2. Find the angle A.

(a) 30°

(b) 45°

(c) 60°

(d) 90°


Answer: (c)

Question 4

The angles of a quadrilateral are in ratio 1:2:3:4. Which angle has the largest measure?

(a) 120°

(b) 144°

(c) 98°

(d) 36°


Answer: (b)

Question 5

The length and breadth of a rectangle is 4 cm and 2 cm respectively. Find the perimeter of the rectangle.

(a) 12 cm

(b) 6 cm

(c) 8 cm

(d) 16 cm


Answer: (a)

Question 6

The diagonals of a rectangle are 2x + 1 and 3x – 1, respectively. Find the value of x.

(a) 1

(b) 2

(c) 3

(d) 3


Answer: (b)

Question 7

The diagonals of a kite:

(a) Bisects each other

(b) Are perpendicular to each other

(c) Does not bisect each other

(d) None of the above


Answer: (b)

Question 8

A rhombus has a side length equal to 5 cm. Find its perimeter.

(a) 25

(b) 10

(c) 20

(d) 30


Answer: (c)

Question 9

ABCD is a parallelogram. If angle A is equal to 45°, then find the measure of its adjacent angle.

(a) 135°

(b) 120°

(c) 115°

(d) 180°


Answer: (a)

Question 10

The kite has exactly two distinct consecutive pairs of sides of equal length.

(a) True

(b) False


Answer: (a)

Question 11

If ∠A and ∠B are two adjacent angles of a parallelogram. If ∠A = 70°, then ∠B = ?

(a) 70°

(b) 90°

(c) 110°

(d) 180°


Answer: (c)

Question 12

ABCD is a rectangle and AC & BD are its diagonals. If AC = 10cm, then BD is:

(a) 10 cm

(b) 5 cm

(c) 15 cm

(d) 20 cm


Answer: (a)

Question 13

Each of the angles of a square is:

(a) Acute angle

(b) Right angle

(c) Obtuse angle

(d) 180 degrees


Answer: (b)
Question 14

Which of the following is not a quadrilateral?

(a) Square

(b) Rectangle

(c) Triangle

(d) Parallelogram


Answer: (c)

Question 15

Which of the following quadrilaterals have two pairs of adjacent sides equal and its diagonals intersect at 90 degrees?

(a) Square

(b) Kite

(c) Rhombus

(d) Rectangle


Answer: (b)

Question 16

If AB and CD are two parallel sides of a parallelogram, then:

(a) AB is greater than CD

(b) AB is less than CD

(c) AB is equal to CD

(d) None of the above


Answer: (c)

Question 17

The quadrilateral whose diagonals are perpendicular to each other is:

(a) Parallelogram

(b) Rectangle

(c) Kite

(d) Rhombus


Answer: (d)

Question 18

The sum of the measures of the exterior angles of any polygon is

(a) 90°

(b) 180°

(c) 360°

(d) 720°


Answer: (c)

Question 19

The measures of the three angles of a quadrilateral are 65°, 75° and 85°. The measure of the fourth angle i65°

(a) 65°

(b) 75°

(c) 85°

(d) 135°


Answer: (d)

Question 20

The measures of each of the four angles of a quadrilateral are equal. Find the measure of each angle.

(a) 45°

(b) 30°

(c) 60°

(d) 90°


Answer: (d)
