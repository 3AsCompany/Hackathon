Chapter 5 Data Handling

Question 1

The width or size of the class interval 30-40 is:

(a) 10

(b) 30

(c) 40

(d) 70


Answer: (a)

Question 2

If a coin is flipped in the air, what is the probability of getting a tail?

(a) 0

(b) 1/2

(c) 1

(d) 2


Answer: (b)

Question 3

A bag has 4 red balls and 4 green balls, what is the probability of getting a red ball randomly?

(a) 1/4

(b) 1/8

(c) 1/2

(d) 0


Answer: (c)

Question 4

If a die is thrown in air, the probability of getting even numbers is:

(a) 1/2

(b) 1/6

(c) 1/3

(d) 2/3


Answer: (c)

Question 5

Pictorial representation of data using symbols is known as:

(a) Bar graph

(b) Pictograph

(c) Pie chart

(d) None of these


Answer: (b)

Question 6

Double bar graphs display ______ sets of data simultaneously.

(a) Four

(b) Three

(c) Two

(d) No


Answer: (c)

Question 7

_________ gives the number of times a particular entry occurs.

(a) Organization of data

(b) Collection of Data

(c) Representation of Data

(d) Frequency distribution table


Answer: (d)

Question 8

In grouped data, each of the group is called:

(a) Class interval

(b) Collection of data

(c) Frequency

(d) Grouped frequency distribution


Answer: (a)

Question 9

If 20-30 is the class interval of grouped data, then lower class limit is:

(a) 50

(b) 30

(c) 20

(d) 10


Answer: (c)

Question 10

The difference between the upper class limit and lower class limit of a class interval is called:

(a) Frequency

(b) Width

(c) Grouped data

(d) Ungrouped data


Answer: (b)
